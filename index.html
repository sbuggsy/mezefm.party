At MezeFM, we believe in the power of music to unite communities, transcend borders, and ignite the spirit. Born from a passion for cultural diversity and the celebration of Eastern European sounds, we strive to create a vibrant space where melodies from distant lands find a home. Our journey began with a simple idea: to share the rich tapestry of Romanian, Bulgarian, Greek, and Serbian music with the world. What started as a humble radio station has evolved into a beacon of cultural exchange, where each tune tells a story and every beat connects hearts. Driven by our commitment to diversity and inclusion, we curate playlists that not only entertain but also educate and inspire. From traditional folk melodies to modern rhythms, our music transcends language barriers and invites listeners on a journey of discovery.

Listening to MezeFM is a breeze! Whether you’re at home, on the go, or at work, our station is accessible through multiple platforms:
  • Website: Tune in directly from our website using the embedded player.
  • Mobile App: Download our app from the App Store or Google Play for seamless streaming on your mobile devices.
  • Smart Speakers: Simply ask your smart speaker to “Play MezeFM” and enjoy our curated playlists instantly.
  • Streaming Platforms: Find us on popular streaming services like TuneIn, iHeartRadio, and more.
